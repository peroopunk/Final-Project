When I'm not daydreaming about my next trip abroad, I'm working diligently as an art director at grafik.com. I draw inspiration from music, my friends and family. In my free time, I enjoy long runs, which counter balances my insane sweet tooth. I've worked with a variety of clients including National Trust or Historic Preservation, Volunteers of America, Cystic Fibrosis Foundation, Honda, the Smithsonian Center for Latino Initiatives and Georgetown University. My work has appeared in Communication Arts, Print, Graphis and the AIGA 50.
